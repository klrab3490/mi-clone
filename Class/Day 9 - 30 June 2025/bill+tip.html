Bill and Tip Calculation

This script calculates the tip based on the bill amount and logs the results.

Tips are calculated as follows:

  • If the bill is between 50 and 300, the tip is 15% of the bill.
  • If the bill is outside this range, the tip is 20% of the bill.

The total amount to be paid is the sum of the bill and the tip.

Check the console for the output of the calculations.

Example Bills

  • Bill 1: $125
  • Bill 2: $555
  • Bill 3: $44
